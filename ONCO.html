Onco App

This is the project to create an application which can be used by doctors practicing Oncology. It will be used to prescribe a treatment to cancer patient based on the symptoms and his/her stage. This Application will help our medical staff to best cater their patients and maintain their register without overhead of written paper work. It will also help to access patient’s previous history, thereby bringing more accuracy in the treatment. This application will be developed and rolled out in multiple phases enhancing the use for doctors/health workers and later by the patients. This application will be made available on the Web as well as Mobile.

Project Span & Duties

1 Year
UX Research
Competitive Analysis

UX Designing & Front End Developer

Problem & Goals

To provide a centralized system for the management of medical records that are accessible from anywhere. So, that the users and doctors could have better management and understanding of patient’s health and diagnosis.
To favor OTP verification through Aadhar. So, that we could maintain a dependable system with security.
To search doctors and hospitals according to the needs of the user and the user could easily refine his/her search according to ratings, reviews, and categories.
To set forth a new and unique feature of updating a patient’s health through a live status update. So, that patient’s family could manage things accordingly.
To allow patients to set reminders for their regular checkups, new appointments, or for medicine dosage.
To provide a linking system for family members, where their profiles are listed with their medical records.

Pain Points

Tedious paperwork.
Keep a track of staffing and technology.
Health and medication tracking and updates
