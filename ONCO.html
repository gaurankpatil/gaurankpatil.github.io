Oncology

Oncology is an user-friendly, evidence-based application for oncologists to prescribe treatment plans for cancer patients based on symptoms, cancer stage, and other clinical parameters. This application will help the medical staff to best cater their patients and maintain their register without overhead of written paper work. It will also help to access patient’s previous history, thereby bringing more accuracy in the treatment. Oncology will be developed and rolled out in multiple phases enhancing the use for doctors/health workers and later by the patients.

Problem Statement

  • Decentralised patient registration process
  • Security
  • Tedious paperwork
  • Keep a track of staffing and technology
  • Health and medication tracking and updates
  • Insurance and reports related

Goal

  • To provide a centralized system to manage all medical records
  • Easy patient registeration process and tracking the entire process
  • Allow patients to set reminders for their regular checkups, new appointments, or for medicine dosage
  • Provide real time health updates
  • Security
