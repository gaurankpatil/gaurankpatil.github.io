Coffee House


The Coffee House app is a convenient way to order ahead for pickup, scan and pay in-store and customize your favorites. Rewards are built right in, so you'll earn Stars towards free drinks and food on your purchases. Customize and place your order, then pick up from a nearby participating store without waiting in line. The app to ease ordering coffee online which in turn will help to save waiting in line times and order coffees quick and easy.

Problem Statement

  • Unable to order coffee online for single or a large group
  • Lot of hassel while picking up orders
  • Long lines for ordering, pickup and payment
  • Scarcity of seating

Goal

  • A faster, more collaborative way make orders
  • Time saving
  • Reserve seating
  • Ease user experience
  • Simplified payment options
  • Error free ordering
  • Order status tracking
