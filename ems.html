Expense Management System


EMS stands for Expense Management System. Expense Management System offers software and solutions to help organizations in digital transformation for assured expense reduction. EMS provides end-to-end suite of solutions in strategic sourcing, procurement and accounts payable for financial control. EMS encorporates all aspects of spend and payment: right from strategic sourcing, supplier management, procurement, e-invoicing, accounts payable, travel & expense management, asset management, imprest (petty cash management), and contracts. EMS allows you keep expenses in check for travellers, expenses and vendors across organizations.

Problem & Goals


The existing product did not help consumers to manage their daily finances effectively and did not provide the necessary insights for consumers to understand their spending patterns, track expenses or even perform budgeting. As a result, the client could neither retain existing customer nor attract new customers. The goal is to create a platform where user can clearly view on financial life. Better understand where money comes and goes with an sequence of visualised reports.

Pain Points


Users are unable to track their expenses.
Applications available in market are difficult to understand.
