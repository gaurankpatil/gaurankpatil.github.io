GreenBay Essentials

Project Overview

Greenbay essentials is an retails store located in New York which sells a variety of GNC products Greenbay Essentials offers a large selection of organic vitamins, all-natural supplements, as well as organic soap and shampoo. Our goal is to make a healthy difference in people’s day to day lives and travel with each customer on their journey to a healthy life.
