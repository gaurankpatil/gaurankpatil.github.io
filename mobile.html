Expenzing Mobile App


Expenzing Travel & Expense is an swift version of the EMS designed and developed to simplify and ease user experience by managing their expenses. This Mobile App is specially designed for clients of Expenzing TNE. Expenzing Mobile Application allows our customers to Create Business Expenses, Travel Requests, Travel Settlements and Submit it for approval. This app offers added functionality of voucher listing, approving, editing functionality and uploading recipts or attachements for easy approval of vouchers. Expenzing Mobile App allows the users add and submit expenses anywhere at anytime. Expenzing Travel & Expense is compatible in all mobile devices and is available on App store and Play Store.
