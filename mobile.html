Mobile App EC Agile

EC Agile is an swift version of the Expense Management System designed and developed to simplify and ease user experience by managing theor expenses. It is a lighter version of the original product from the user perspective of the application incorporating the improvents in the overall design and feel of the application. This app offers added functionality of listing the vouchers, approving and editing functionality. Also has the feature of capturing the recipts and storing them as necessary attachements for easy approval of vouchers. EC Agile is developed for both IOS and Android and supports multilanguage functionality for international users.
