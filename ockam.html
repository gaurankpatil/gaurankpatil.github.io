Ockam RenderSwarm Farm

RenderSwarm was an in-house project launched by Ockam System for showcasing their work and efforts as an biggest render farm in India. The logo is designed in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop which depicts the teams positive energy and enthusiasm like a honey bee working 24/7 day in and day out to meet their goals. RENDERswarm has an outstanding in-house team. With their vast experience and knowledge, they cater to all our clients needs and support. You just need to submit your scene files and they will do the rest. They will provide you with, the static rendered frames on request. Also they will deliver the final sequence renders on to your predefined output path. Your render wranglers are free to do in-house work. This is a major value-add in comparison to the other cloud providers, where one wrangler is required to interact with their UI 24X7 to get jobs done. The website is developed and managed in Wordpress in a month tenure using customized theme and plugins. An compeletely responsive and SEO optimized website built for ease to access on all devices. RenderSwarm has a provided their services for a number of clients among in which the current they worked on is Chhota Bheem Kungfu Dhamaka and many more.
