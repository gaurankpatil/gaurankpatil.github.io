Yo-Yo App


Yo-Yo App allows the coaches of any sport teams entering test results of players single or in a team. This app is is used by coaches for working with athletes Yo-Yo App allows you to run any type of Multi-Stage Fitness Test (MSFT) for any sport, record scores for teams, setup multiple teams/groups of players and view previous scores on a graph to track player and team fitness over the season. The Yo-Yo Intermittent Test is a fitness test designed to simulate the intermittent nature of certain sporting activities such as soccer and many other forms of football. Right now, coaches are writing down the test result on a physical piece of paper and later entering results in a web application. This is very time consuming and inefficient. This app is designed in order to make it easier for coaches and to display the test result to other persons (e.g. athletes, parents to athletes, etc.).

The Problem & Goals


Unable to conduct and record fast and easy athletes test results which results in an very inefficient and tedious approach. An app to ease the task of creating and assign athletes to a Yo-Yo test and also help the Coach entering test results for a Yo-Yo test.

Pain Points


Unable to enter Yo-Yo test results fast and easy.
Currently using an Inefficient method of writing down on paper and making notes
Unable to create new test or create reports of the previous tests conducted
