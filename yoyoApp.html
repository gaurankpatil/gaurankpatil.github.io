Yo-Yo App


Yo-Yo App allows the coaches of any sport teams entering test results of players single or in a team. This app is is used by coaches for working with athletes Yo-Yo App allows you to run any type of Multi-Stage Fitness Test (MSFT) for any sport, record scores for teams, setup multiple teams/groups of players and view previous scores on a graph to track player and team fitness over the season. The Yo-Yo Intermittent Test is a fitness test designed to simulate the intermittent nature of certain sporting activities such as soccer and many other forms of football. Right now, coaches are writing down the test result on a physical piece of paper and later entering results in a web application. This is very time consuming and inefficient. This app is designed in order to make it easier for coaches and to display the test result to other persons (e.g. athletes, parents to athletes, etc.).

Problem Statement

Coaches of sports teams are manually recording fitness test results on paper and later entering them into a web application. This process is inefficient, time-consuming, and prone to errors. Coaches need a faster and more streamlined way to record and track players' test results, set up teams, and monitor fitness progress throughout the season. Additionally, there is no easy way to share these results with relevant parties such as athletes and their parents, which can hinder timely communication and analysis. There is a need for a digital solution that simplifies the recording, tracking, and sharing of fitness test results, enabling coaches to efficiently manage multiple teams and monitor players' performance over time.

Target Audience

  • Coaches of Sports Teams: Users who conduct fitness tests for their athletes.
  • Athletes: Users whose fitness results are being tracked.
  • Sports Teams / Organizations: Administrators and management staff overseeing multiple teams and players.
  • Fitness Trainers and Sports Academies: Trainers who may use the app to conduct fitness tests and monitor athlete performance outside of traditional team sports.

Goal & Objectives

  • Simplify Data Entry for Coaches: Eliminate the need for manual recording and separate data entry by providing an easy-to-use digital interface for entering fitness test results in real-time, directly during testing.
  • Track and Visualize Player and Team Performance: Provide a visual dashboard or graphs for coaches to view individual and team fitness progress over the season, allowing them to make informed decisions about training and player development.
  • Support Multiple Sports and Fitness Tests: Allow the app to be adaptable for various sports and fitness tests, especially for sports requiring intermittent fitness like soccer, football, and others, making it a versatile tool for coaches across multiple disciplines.
  • Manage Multiple Teams and Groups: Enable coaches to easily create and manage multiple teams or groups of athletes within the app, with the ability to assign tests and track results for each group separately.
  • Improve Communication with Athletes and Other Stakeholders: Facilitate the sharing of test results with athletes, parents, and other relevant parties, giving them access to view progress and understand areas for improvement.
  • Increase Efficiency and Reduce Time Spent on Administration: Streamline the entire fitness testing process, saving coaches time by reducing the need for duplicate data entry and paperwork, and allowing them to focus more on coaching and athlete development.
  • Ensure Data Accuracy and Accessibility: Provide a secure, reliable platform that ensures accurate recording of fitness data, which can be accessed anytime by coaches, athletes, and other authorized users to track historical performance.
